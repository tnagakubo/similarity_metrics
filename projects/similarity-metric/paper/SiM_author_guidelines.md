## Manuscript Requirements

### Abstract
- **Length**: Up to **250 words** (except book reviews)
- **Content**: Concise summary of the whole paper, not just conclusions
- **Style**: Understandable without reference to the rest of the paper
- **Citations**: No citations to other published work allowed in abstract

### Title
- **Full title**: Required
- **Short title**: Up to **70 characters**

### Keywords
- Up to **6 keywords** for indexing purposes

### Authors
- Full names and affiliations of all authors
- Corresponding author: full address, email, telephone, fax

---

## Reference Format

- **Style**: Numbered consecutively in order of appearance
- **In-text citation**: Arabic superscript numerals (e.g., text^1^)
- **Journal abbreviations**: Follow Chemical Abstracts conventions
- **Completeness**: References should be as complete as possible

### Example Format
```
1. Author AB, Author CD. Title of article. Journal Abbrev. Year;Volume(Issue):Pages.
```

## Figures and Tables

### Figures
- **Format**: .tiff or .eps
- **Submission**: Each figure as separate file
- **Labeling**: Lead author's name, figure number, top of figure indicated

### Tables
- Standard formatting per journal style

## Manuscript Length (Estimated from Literature)

Statistics in Medicine does not specify strict word limits, but based on published methodology papers:

| Section | Typical Length |
|---------|---------------|
| Abstract | 200-250 words |
| Introduction | 500-800 words |
| Methods | 1,000-2,000 words |
| Results | 1,000-1,500 words |
| Discussion | 600-1,000 words |
| **Total** | **4,000-6,000 words** |

**Note**: Methodology papers may have longer Methods sections.

## Submission Process

1. Submit via **Research Exchange** portal
2. Online peer review system
3. Track progress via web interface

### Requirements
- Original unpublished work
- Not under consideration elsewhere
- All authors must approve submission

## Proofs

- Publisher supplies proofs to corresponding author
- **Turnaround**: Ideally within 2 days
- Purpose: Correct production errors only
