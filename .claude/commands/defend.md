# Research Lab: Defend

Expert homage attacks a claim, team defends.
Claim: $ARGUMENTS

## Execution

1. **Harvey**: State claim to defend
2. **Louis**: Select attacking expert style
3. Expert homage attacks the claim
4. **Mike/Harvey**: Defend
5. **Louis**: Judge defense quality
6. **Donna**: Add scene to SUITS.md

## SUITS.md Scene (add at TOP)

```markdown
### [YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM] Scene: Defense Exercise

**INT. PEARSON SPECTER LITT - CONFERENCE ROOM - DAY**

*Harvey stands at one end. A visiting expert (homage) sits opposite. Mike has his notepad ready.*

**Harvey**: （腕を組んで）
「この主張を defend する：{claim}」

**Dr. Richter** (Wasserman-style): （鋭い目で）
「その主張は疑問だ。{attack}。
なぜシンプルな方法では駄目なのか？」

「反論します。{defense logic}」

*Louis watches carefully, making notes.*

**Dr. Richter**: 
「それでは不十分だ。{follow-up attack}」

**Harvey**: （割り込んで）
「なら、こう考えろ。{stronger defense}」

*Louis nods slowly.*

**Louis**: （メモを見て）
「Defense Success。この論理を Discussion に入れろ。
査読者が同じ攻撃をしてきたら、これで返せる」

```

## Attacker Style Selection

| Claim Type | Best Attacker Style |
|------------|-------------------|
| Method superiority | Wasserman-style |
| Mathematical proof | Rao-style, Bickel-style |
| Bayesian approach | Gelman-style |
| Causal claim | Rubin-style |
| Clinical application | Senn-style |

## Important

- Attacks should be **relentless**
- Defense logic goes into the paper
- Prepares for real reviewer attacks
