# Research Lab: Simulate Q&A

Simulate post-presentation Q&A with legendary statistician homages as audience.
Conference: $ARGUMENTS (e.g., JSM, ISBA, ENAR)

## Execution

1. **Harvey/Mike**: Present key points (3 min summary)
2. Generate 2-3 audience members with expert styles
3. Audience asks questions
4. Presenter responds
5. **Louis**: Evaluate responses
6. **Donna**: Add scene to SUITS.md

## SUITS.md Scene (add at TOP)

