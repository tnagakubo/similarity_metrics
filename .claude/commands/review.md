# Research Lab: Review

Louis reviews the paper draft critically.

## Execution

1. **Louis**: Review entire draft
2. **Louis**: List issues by severity (Critical/Major/Minor)
3. **Louis**: Provide revision suggestions
4. **Donna**: Add scene to SUITS.md

## Severity Definitions

- **Critical**: Paper will be rejected without fix
- **Major**: Will receive major revision request
- **Minor**: Desirable but not blocking
